Donation steps, preparations, and restrictions:

Let's begin our journey to learn more about donation and how to donate. Before we start donating, I want to first give you some information about blood... and then we'll begin our journey to donate

Restrictions on donation•♡♡

(Who Cannot Donate?)

  • People with infectious diseases such as (hepatitis or AIDS).
  • Those who have recently tattoo or body piercing (they must wait months).
  • Those who have recently udergone major surgeries or treatments.
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women.
  • Those who have travelers to areas endemic with malaria or dengue fever.

Health Preparation and screening•♡♡

Health preparation before donation••♡

  • Eat a light, healthy meal 2 hours before donation (fruits, whole grain bread)
  • Drink 2-3 glasses of water to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid excessive caffeine.
  • Sleep 7-8 hours for best body condition.

Donation Frequency•♡♡

  • Men: Can donate every 3 months.
  • Women: Can donate every 4 months.
  • Note:Blood donation is very safe when donors follow the health requirements. These rules are not restrictions — they are guarantees to protect both the donor and the patient.

What to Avoid befor donation•♡♡

  • Smoking for at least two hours before donation.
  • Strenuous exercise right before donation.
  • Drinking alcohol or unhealthy substances within 24 hours befor donation.


On Donation Day•♡♡

  • Check for headaches, dizziness, or fatigue.
  • Monitor your blood pressure.
  • Inform the team about any medications.
  • Ensure you are free from colds or infections.
